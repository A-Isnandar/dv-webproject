F&B and Admission

  • Outside Food & Drinks: Strictly prohibited. Corkage fees apply for bottles brought from outside.
  • Age Policy: Children welcome until 9 PM. After 9 PM, strictly 21+ only.

Guest Etiquette

  • Aggressive, disruptive, or excessively intimate behavior is not tolerated.
  • Please dispose of trash and hygiene products in the bins provided.
  • Respect other guests' privacy and space.

Safety & Liability

  • No Lifeguard: Supervise children at all times, especially near water.
  • Caution: Floors may be slippery near pool areas.
  • Guests are responsible for any damage to facilities or property.

Zero Tolerance

  • Possession of illegal drugs and weapons is strictly prohibited (Indonesian Law).
  • Security reserves the right to refuse entry or remove guests violating rules.

Attire & Towels

  • Proper Swimwear: Mandatory. No jeans or street clothes.
  • Towels: No personal towels allowed. Rentals available (IDR 40K++).

Safety & Children

  • No Lifeguard: Swim at your own risk.
  • Children under 6 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • No running, pushing, or rough play.

Hygiene Rules

  • Rinse at shower before swimming.
  • Do not swim with open wounds.
  • No spitting or urinating in the pool.

Restrictions & Info

  • No large floaties allowed.
  • Only swim; no wading or walking in lanes.
  • Depth: Adult Pool 1.2M | Kids Pool 50CM.

Visiting Hours

  • Mon - Thu: Welcome in designated areas (All day).
  • Fri - Sun & Holidays: Allowed ONLY from 7 AM to 12 PM.

Strict Requirements

  • Pets must be on a leash at all times.
  • Must wear diapers.
  • Owners must bring their own plastic bags and clean up waste.
  • Pets allowed in dedicated areas only.
